I. SCOPE OF REPRESENTATION

The Firm agrees to provide Executive Client Service, which establishes a standing attorney–client relationship independent of any single case or proceeding.

Executive Client Service includes, but is not limited to:

• Priority handling of all legal matters
• Direct access to senior and partner-level counsel
• Immediate response to arrests, investigations, or enforcement actions
• Ongoing legal advisory and risk oversight
• Preferred scheduling for consultations, hearings, and negotiations
• Allocation of firm resources on a priority basis

The Firm retains discretion over staffing, strategy, and allocation of resources.

Not Automatically Included:

Out-of-scope matters may require additional retainers or separate agreements.
